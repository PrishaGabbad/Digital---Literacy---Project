THE BREIF: You have just been selected as a Student Digital Ambassador at your college. Your role is to help your peers navigate the digital world — from creating professional online profiles, to communicating safely and responsibly online. Your first assignment is to build a Digital Literacy Portfolio that you will present to the faculty committee and share with your batch.

Project Overview

This project is based on the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students in today’s digital world. It focuses on developing practical skills such as using online platforms, creating professional profiles, and communicating effectively through digital tools. The project also emphasizes safe and responsible use of the internet. Through these tasks, I gained hands-on experience with various tools and improved my understanding of digital environments.

Task 1: Infographic:

In this task, I created an infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy in a simple and visually appealing way. The infographic includes topics such as digital tools, safe internet practices, and email etiquette. I focused on making the design clean and easy to understand using icons and short text. This task helped me improve my presentation and design skills.
